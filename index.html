Clean Energy

Cobra is committed to a clean, sustainable future.

Nuclear Power, fuelled by Uranium is more & more recognised as a clean energy source. It accounts for approximately 11 percent of the world's electricity requirements and provides a carbon-free, efficient, reliable and abundant source of base-load electricity.

Located in Namibia, Africa’s leading Uranium producing country, Cobra Resources are dedicated to exploring and de-risking future energy supplies. We are positioning ready to supply new sources of Uranium to key global energy markets as energy demands grow.

Rössing North Deposit

As the leading operation of Cobra Resources, the Rössing North Uranium Project is located directly adjacent to the Rio Tinto Rössing Mine (12% of World Production).

Having already delineated 9Mlbs of U3O8 in the 2015 Maiden Resource, we will now look to grow the resource from the 180Mlb Exploration Target.
